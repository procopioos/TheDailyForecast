# Meet Sunny

*Your personal weather companion, right inside the forecast.*

---

## Meet Sunny

Sunny is a friendly AI assistant built directly into The Daily Forecast. It knows your current weather conditions in real time and can help you make the most of every day — whether you're deciding what to wear, planning a weekend hike, or just wondering if it's a good day for a walk.

## What Can Sunny Do?

- **Weather Q&A** — ask anything about the current conditions. Sunny reads your temperature, humidity, wind, UV index, and more to give you answers that actually make sense.
- **Outfit suggestions** — not sure what to wear? Sunny considers the full picture — temperature, rain, wind, and sun — to recommend something comfortable and appropriate.
- **Activity ideas** — from jogging to museum visits, Sunny helps you plan your day based on what the weather is really doing outside.
- **Lifestyle tips** — best time for a coffee outside? What to cook on a rainy afternoon? Sunny's got you covered.

## Runs Entirely on Your Mac

Sunny doesn't send anything to external servers. All conversations and AI processing happen right on your device, keeping your data completely private. Your weather, your conversations — they never leave your Mac.

The panel adapts to match the current weather too — warm tones on sunny days, cool blues when it rains. It feels like a natural part of your forecast.

## Availability

Sunny is available on **macOS 26 (Tahoe)** and later, on **Apple Silicon** Macs (M1 or newer). On older systems, The Daily Forecast works just as beautifully — Sunny simply waits until your Mac is ready.
