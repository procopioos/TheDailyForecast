# The Daily Forecast

*Your forecast, now beautifully open-sourced.*

A clean, minimal weather app for macOS that puts your forecast front and center.

---

## Features

- **Live weather at a glance** — temperature, humidity, wind speed, and UV index, updated automatically
- **City search** — find any city in the world with instant autocomplete
- **Celsius or Fahrenheit** — switch between units in one tap
- **Sunny** — a built-in on-device AI assistant that knows your weather and can suggest outfits, activities, and more ([meet Sunny](Documentation/Sunny/IntroducingSunny.md))
- **Condition-themed design** — the entire interface adapts its colors and mood to match the current weather outside

---

## Getting Started

1. Open `TheDailyForecast.xcodeproj` in Xcode
2. Hit **Build** and run
3. That's it — enjoy your forecast

Requires **macOS 14** (Sonoma) or later.  
Sunny AI requires **macOS 26** (Tahoe) and Apple Silicon (M1 or later).
